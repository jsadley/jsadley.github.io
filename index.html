Guns Are Killing Us

Here are three reasons not to own a gun.

1. Guns don't make you safer.

A study found that handgun purchasers were ten times more likely to die from a violent death. Owning a gun may make you feel safe, but really it makes you far more likely to die.

2. Guns don't make the rest of us safer.

According to a study, firearm homicide rates were nineteen times higher in the U.S. than other high-income countries. For 15-year olds to 24-year olds, firearm homicide rates were over forty times higher. Guns are killing us.

3. Guns make you look like a coward.

Possessing an instrument that increases your own risk of death and increases other people's risk of death can only be justified by people who are overcome by fear.

What you can do about it.

  • Tell everyone you know that guns are a tragedy.
  • Re-tweet @AnotherGunDeath to let the world know how many accidental gun deaths occur.
